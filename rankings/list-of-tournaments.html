For a tournament to be considered for MSQBRank, it must

  • Use powers (of any amount) and negs (of any amount).
  • Use three-part-bonuses
  • Publish the number of powers, negs, and bonus points scored, or make it possible to determine any of those three values.
  • List what set(s) it is using, and if it is using multiple, must make it possible to determine what games were on what set.
  • Publish team stats, attributing the right stats to the right team.
  • Have had at least 60 tossups heard by at least two teams.
  • Not have faced logistical problems that may have caused issues with statistics.
  • have at least two middle schools in attendance 
  • have a certain school play at least three other middle schools (these three schools do not have to be unique) and hear at least 60 tossups against another middle school
