Welcome to our April (pre-nats) rankings! MSNCT is coming this weekend, and we are very excited to be presenting our rankings. These rankings include all the eligible tournaments before MSNCT, so it is our best outlook before nationals!

Team poll results are out on the “Polls” section of our website.

We will be doing things a bit differently since MSNCT is coming up, most notably, we have temporarily replaced “Region Ranking” with “MSNCT Ranking.” A “1” indicates the team is the best team going in our ranking system, “2” indicates the second best, etc. “N/A” indicates a team not going to nationals.

We will be releasing a final May ranking with MSNCT stats and possibly other post-MSNCT tournaments. This month, we will also be releasing “Hard Set Rankings,” rankings if MS sets + CERES + SCOP MS were not included, full rankings (of all ~500 teams in our database!), and MSNCT rankings — just the teams going to MSNCT. You may view those rankings here.

As usual, we will only be presenting the top 100 teams, but if you would like to see more, you are encouraged to check out the sheet linked above!
